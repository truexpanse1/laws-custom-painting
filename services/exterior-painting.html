Our Exterior Process

How We Paint Your Exterior

Exterior painting demands thorough preparation. Here’s how we ensure lasting results.

1

Inspection

We examine every surface for damage, rot, peeling, and areas needing repair.

2

Power Wash

We clean all surfaces to remove dirt, mildew, and loose paint for proper adhesion.

3

Repair & Prep

Caulking, scraping, sanding, priming—we prepare every inch for paint.

4

Application

Multiple coats of premium exterior paint applied with brushes, rollers, and spray.

5

Inspection

We walk the entire exterior with you to ensure every detail meets our standards.
