What We Do

Expert Cabinet Refinishing Services

A full kitchen renovation can cost $30,000–$80,000 and take months. Cabinet refinishing gives you the same dramatic transformation for a fraction of the price in just 5–7 days. We strip, sand, prime, and apply multiple coats of premium cabinet-grade paint or stain for a factory-smooth finish that looks and feels like new custom cabinetry.

The Transformation

From Outdated to Outstanding

Most kitchens we see have solid, well-built cabinets—they just look tired, dated, or the wrong color. Instead of spending tens of thousands on new cabinets, refinishing lets you keep your existing layout and hardware while completely transforming the look and feel of your kitchen.

Our process delivers a factory-smooth finish that’s durable enough to withstand daily use for years. No brush marks, no drips, no uneven coverage—just a beautiful, professional result that makes your kitchen feel brand new.

FAQ

Cabinet Refinishing Questions

Most kitchen cabinet projects take 5–7 business days from start to finish. We work efficiently while allowing proper dry time between coats for the best results.

You’ll have limited access to your kitchen during the project. We recommend setting up a temporary kitchen area. We’ll work with you to minimize disruption as much as possible.

Cabinet refinishing typically costs 60–80% less than new cabinets. A full kitchen refinishing usually runs $3,500–$8,000 compared to $20,000–$60,000+ for new custom cabinets.

We can refinish most cabinet types including solid wood, MDF, and thermofoil (once the laminate is removed). During our free consultation, we’ll assess your cabinets and let you know if refinishing is the right option.

Very durable. We use professional cabinet-grade paints and finishes designed to withstand daily use, including the heat and moisture common in kitchens. Our finishes resist chipping, scratching, and yellowing.
