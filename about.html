About Us

Where Quality Meets Artistry

For over 30 years, Law’s Custom Painting has been transforming homes and businesses across Yuba City and Sutter County with premium craftsmanship and unwavering dedication.

Our Story

30+ Years of Painting Excellence

In 1994, John Law picked up a brush and made a promise: every surface he touched would be a masterpiece. What started as one man with a vision has grown into Yuba City’s most trusted painting company—but the promise has never changed.

Over three decades, John has personally overseen more than 5,000 painting projects across Sutter County. From the smallest accent wall to the largest commercial building, every project gets the same meticulous attention to detail that built our reputation.

Today, Law’s Custom Painting is still family-owned and operated. We’re not a franchise. We’re not a corporate chain. We’re your neighbors—and we take personal pride in every home and business we paint.

Our Values

Family-Owned. Community-Driven.

Being a family-owned business means we operate differently than the big corporate painting companies. When you work with us, you work directly with John and his handpicked crew—not a rotating cast of subcontractors.

  • Integrity: We do what we say we’ll do, when we say we’ll do it
  • Craftsmanship: We never cut corners on prep, materials, or technique
  • Respect: We treat your home like it’s our own
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no games
  • Accountability: If something isn’t right, we make it right—period
